Campus Life

  • Library

    A Library is a place where a large number of books are stored, collected and offered for reference and borrowing. Library of THE Vivek High SCHOOL is a special one for children as it contains many different story books for children, some teach us something, some make us laugh and some make us happy. And in the library if we can't find any book the librarian gives us.

  • Auditorium

    Auditorium is a place to which people may come to observe a sports event or performance. An example of an auditorium is the large room in which parents may gather to watch their childs musical performance at school

  • Play Ground

    A play ground is a place where everyone plays. In The Vivek High School their are many sports to choose from like cricket, football, skating, badminton, basketball,etc. Each sport is played where it has to be played like cricket and foot ball in the ground basketball in the basketball court and skating in the skating rink.
